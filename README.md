## 📌 Project Overview
The Insurance Report Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze insurance policy performance, customer demographics, premium trends, and claim analysis using real-world styled insurance data.

## 🏢 Business Problem
Insurance companies generate large volumes of policy and claim data daily. Without proper visualization and reporting systems, it becomes difficult to track premium performance, customer insights, claim trends, and policy effectiveness.

This dashboard solves that problem by converting raw insurance data into meaningful business insights through interactive reports and KPI monitoring.

## 💼 Business Insights
- Identified high-premium insurance policy categories
- Analyzed claim approval, rejection, and pending trends
- Tracked customer demographics across insurance plans
- Evaluated policy coverage versus claim amount
- Improved visibility into insurance business performance

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze insurance premium and coverage trends
- Monitor claim amount and claim status performance
- Understand customer demographics and policy behavior
- Track policy distribution across insurance categories
- Create interactive and business-friendly visual reports
